Overview

SpongeBob SquarePants is an American animated series created by Stephen Hillenburg. The show follows SpongeBob, a cheerful and optimistic sea sponge who lives in a pineapple house under the sea in Bikini Bottom.

First aired in 1999, SpongeBob quickly became one of the most iconic cartoon characters in television history. His daily adventures often include his best friend Patrick Star, his neighbor Squidward Tentacles, and his job at the Krusty Krab.

SpongeBob is known for his positive attitude, kindness, and endless enthusiasm. Although he often gets into silly or chaotic situations, his heart is always in the right place. Over the years, the series has gained millions of fans around the world.
