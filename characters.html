Characters

SpongeBob SquarePants

A cheerful and optimistic sea sponge who works at the Krusty Krab. Known for his kindness, laughter, and never-ending enthusiasm.

Patrick Star

SpongeBob’s best friend. A lovable, funny, and sometimes clueless pink starfish who lives under a rock.

Squidward Tentacles

SpongeBob’s grumpy neighbor and co-worker. He loves music, art, and peace—things he rarely gets because of SpongeBob.

Mr. Krabs

The money-loving owner of the Krusty Krab. He cares about his business, money... and sometimes his employees.

Sandy Cheeks

A brave and intelligent squirrel from Texas who lives underwater in a special dome. Skilled in karate and science.
